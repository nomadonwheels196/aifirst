# Customer Case Study: Life as Code

**Industry**: Technology / Software Consulting
**User Profile**: Founder/CEO with ADHD, managing personal and business information
**Challenge**: Information chaos preventing productivity and causing decision paralysis
**Solution**: AI-guided life organization system with automated backups
**Time to Value**: 2 hours
**Outcome**: Hundreds of hours saved, significantly reduced stress, complete peace of mind

---

## The Challenge

Our founder, Travis, faced a problem common to knowledge workers and executives: **information scattered everywhere.**

- Medical receipts needed for taxes lost in multiple folders
- Company legal documents in one location, operational docs in another
- Workshop manuals for personal projects mixed with business files
- No consistent backup strategy
- No clear filing system
- Constant searching for the "right document"

For someone managing both a growing company and a complex personal life, this wasn't just annoying—it was paralyzing. **Hours wasted searching. Opportunities missed. Stress compounded.**

The bigger problem? **He didn't know how to fix it.** Should files live in cloud? Local? Both? What folder structure makes sense? How do you migrate years of files without getting overwhelmed?

## The Traditional Approach (What Doesn't Work)

Most people try:

1. **Hiring someone to organize**: Expensive, doesn't scale, person leaves and chaos returns
2. **Productivity consultants**: Give advice, but don't do the work. Implementation is on you.
3. **Software tools (Notion, DEVONthink, etc.)**: Require significant upfront learning and ongoing maintenance
4. **"I'll do it this weekend"**: Narrator: *He never did it that weekend.*

The problem with all these approaches? **They transfer the burden to you.** You have to figure out the system. You have to implement it. You have to maintain it.

## The AI First Approach

Travis opened Claude Code and had a conversation. Not a command. Not a form to fill out. **A genuine collaboration.**

He said: *"I believe in the concept of life as code, but I'm not sure how to organize it."*

And the AI responded not with a solution, but with **questions**:

- "What matters most to you—speed of access or automatic backups?"
- "Should we move everything at once or build structure first?"
- "You mentioned ADHD—should we design for that?"

Over two hours, they:

1. **Discussed trade-offs** (home directory vs. Documents folder)
2. **Made strategic decisions** (incremental migration, not big-bang)
3. **Built a complete system**:
   - Clean folder structure (work/personal/archive)
   - Git repositories with smart configurations
   - Automated daily backups (GitHub + NAS)
   - Comprehensive documentation
4. **Tested and verified** everything worked

## The Result

**Immediate Impact**:
- Complete life organization system operational in 2 hours
- 167 files properly organized
- Automated backups running (4 AM daily)
- Zero manual work required going forward

**Ongoing Benefits**:
- Hundreds of hours saved per year (no more searching)
- Tax season no longer a nightmare (receipts auto-organized with tags)
- Company documents safe and version-controlled
- Cross-machine sync automatic (work computer, home computer, shared with team)

**Most Important**:
