# Glossary — Spain Public Sector Procurement (English ↔ Spanish)

- Public Administration — Administración Pública (AAPP): Spanish government bodies (central, regional, local) and public sector entities.
- Public procurement law — Ley de Contratos del Sector Público (LCSP): core law governing how AAPP buy goods/services.
- Tender — Licitación; Call for bids — Convocatoria/Anuncio de licitación; RFP package — Pliegos.
- Specifications — Pliegos: split into Administrative Terms — Pliego de Cláusulas Administrativas Particulares (PCAP) and Technical Specs — Pliego de Prescripciones Técnicas (PPT/PPTP).
- Contracting authority — Órgano de Contratación; Contracting body — Mesa de contratación (evaluation committee).
- Vendor registration — Registro de licitadores (ROLECE): optional/beneficial pre-qualification registry.
- Procurement platform — Plataforma de Contratación del Sector Público (PLACSP): public portal for tenders and submissions.
- Economic solvency — Solvencia económica y financiera: e.g., minimum turnover, insurance.
- Technical solvency — Solvencia técnica o profesional: e.g., references, staff qualifications, certifications.
- Classification — Clasificación empresarial: mandatory only for certain works/services; often not required for standard SaaS.
- Award criteria — Criterios de adjudicación: price/quality weighting; MEAT — OFERTA económicamente más ventajosa.
- Security framework — Esquema Nacional de Seguridad (ENS): national information security requirements; levels: Básico, Medio, Alto.
- Interoperability — Esquema Nacional de Interoperabilidad (ENI): data formats, e-signatures, records management.
- Accessibility — RD 1112/2018; EN 301 549: accessibility standard for public sector websites/apps (WCAG 2.1 AA alignment).
- Data protection — RGPD/GDPR + LOPDGDD: EU and Spanish data protection laws; Data Processing Agreement — Encargo de tratamiento.
- E-invoicing — Facturae format; FACe platform: B2G electronic invoice submission.
- Qualified certificates — Certificados cualificados (eIDAS): used for signing/submitting bids; trust list maintained by EU.
- Certification body — Entidad de certificación; ENAC: Spanish accreditation body for ENS and ISO audits.
- CCN-CERT — National Cryptology Center: publishes ENS guidance (CCN-STIC series).
- NIS2 — EU cybersecurity directive affecting essential/important entities; may impact cloud providers over time.

Notes
- “Pliegos” are the binding docs. If a pliego requires ENS “Medio” certification and ISO 27001, that becomes the contract requirement.
- Many processes require a digital certificate for the legal representative to sign and submit documents on PLACSP.
