## Resolution

- Single Capital Expenditure: from USD/EUR 250,000 to USD/EUR [•] for any single unbudgeted capital expenditure.

FURTHER RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are authorized and directed to prepare and file any amendments or side letters necessary to reflect these adjustments in the Company’s governing documents, including the Shareholders Agreement and Articles, and to obtain any required approvals, including the separate consent of holders of a majority of Class B shares for any Reserved Matters.

FURTHER RESOLVED, that any actions heretofore taken by the officers of the Company in connection with the foregoing are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.
