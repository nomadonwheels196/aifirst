# Board Consent — Founder Working Capital Loan Facility

Company: Ai‑First Holdings Ltd. (Cayman)
Date: [Date]

The undersigned, constituting all members of the Board of Directors of the Company, hereby adopt the following resolutions by written consent.

## 1. Approval of Founder Loan Facility
RESOLVED, that the Company establish a related‑party, unsecured working capital loan facility (the “Facility”) from the Founder(s) listed in Schedule A, on the following terms:
- Maximum aggregate principal: USD $[Cap]
- Draws: from time to time upon CFO request, up to the Cap
- Interest: AFR (Applicable Federal Rate) for short‑term loans (compounded annually)
- Maturity: [12/18/24] months from each draw date (earlier upon Qualified Financing or mutual agreement)
- Prepayment: Permitted at any time without penalty
- Subordination: Subordinate to institutional debt (if any), per reasonable subordination agreement
- Use of proceeds: General working capital
- Governing law: [Cayman/Delaware]

RESOLVED FURTHER, that the officers are authorized to execute promissory notes substantially in the form attached as Exhibit 1, issue draw notices, and take any actions necessary to implement the Facility, including bookkeeping entries and repayments.

## 2. Related‑Party Approval; Best Interest
RESOLVED, that the Board determines the Facility is in the best interests of the Company, on arm’s‑length‑equivalent terms; any conflicted directors abstained from voting.

## 3. ESA / CIGA Notation (Cayman)
RESOLVED, that approval of the Facility be recorded in the ESA evidence log; the Board will minute funding and repayments at each Cayman ESA meeting as applicable.

## 4. Ratification
RESOLVED, that all actions by the officers consistent with the foregoing are ratified and approved.
