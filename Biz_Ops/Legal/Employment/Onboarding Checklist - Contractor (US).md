# Onboarding Checklist — Contractor (US)

Created by: Travis Sheppard
Category: HR & Employment — Checklists
Status: Template

## Documents to Execute
- US Contractor IP Assignment and Confidentiality Agreement
  (`ExportBlock-…/Employment/US Contractor IP Assignment and Confidentiality Agreement.md`)
- Statement of Work (SOW) — US Contractor (this checklist references the SOW template)
- Confidentiality/Security Policy Acknowledgment (internal policy)
- DPA (only if contractor processes personal data as a processor)

## Tax and Pay Setup
- W‑9 (US taxpayer) or W‑8BEN‑E (entity, foreign) / W‑8BEN (individual, foreign)
- ACH/Wire banking details; remit‑to address
- Confirm 1099 classification and thresholds

## Access & Security
- Create vendor account; least‑privilege access to tools/repos
- Issue Company credentials; enforce MFA
- Share secure data handling and incident reporting procedures

## SOW Scope & Acceptance
- Define deliverables, milestones, acceptance criteria, and change‑order process
- Set fees (fixed/time‑and‑materials), invoicing schedule, and net terms
- Identify key personnel; no subcontracting without consent

## IP & Confidentiality
- Confirm all deliverables fall under work‑made‑for‑hire/assignment terms
- Verify pre‑existing materials listed in Exhibit A with license back
- Ensure intercompany chain to Ai‑First Holdings (Cayman) referenced

## Compliance
- Export controls / sanctions screening if applicable
- Insurance certs if required (e.g., professional liability)
