# Sales

## Purpose
Define and operate the end-to-end sales motion: ICP targeting, discovery, solutions, proposals, and close. Centralizes playbooks, collateral, and customer stories.

## Structure
- `Case_Studies/` — published and draft stories (see README.md)
- `Sales_Process/` — sales stages and exit criteria (folder name has a typo; consider `Sales_Process/`)

## Current State
- Last Updated: 2025-11-07
- Status: active
- DRI: Sales Lead

## Process (high-level)
- Stages: Lead → MQL → Discovery → Solution Fit → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed (Won/Lost)
- Exit Criteria: defined for each stage in `Sales_Process/README.md:1`
- Artifacts: discovery notes, ROI calculator, proposal/SoW, security responses

## Inputs
- ICP/personas, product/pricing, competitive intel
- Marketing-sourced demand; partner referrals

## Outputs
- Qualified opportunities and closed-won revenue
- Case studies and referenceable customers

## Security & Privacy
