# Title Slide Image Prompt for Ai First

**For use with:** Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, or professional AI image generators

---

## Primary Image Prompt (Recommended)

```
A professional diverse office worker confidently working at their desk with a subtle holographic AI assistant interface gently floating beside them, providing contextual guidance. The person is actively engaged in real work (laptop, documents visible), looking empowered and capable, with a slight smile suggesting a breakthrough moment. The AI element is minimal and translucent—suggesting support, not replacement. Modern office environment with warm natural lighting from a window. The composition emphasizes the human at the center, technology as elegant support. Color palette: warm teals and blues with natural tones. Style: clean, modern, professional photography with subtle futuristic elements. 8k, high detail, cinematic composition, shallow depth of field focusing on the person.

Visual metaphor: Human potential amplified, not replaced.

Avoid: robots, generic corporate stock photos, cold technology, disconnected screens, impersonal atmospheres.
```

## Technical Specifications

**For all options:**

- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (standard presentation format)
- **Resolution:** Minimum 4K (3840 x 2160) for crisp projection
- **Style:** Professional photography with subtle digital enhancement
- **Lighting:** Natural, warm, aspirational (not harsh or cold)
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Warm teals, professional blues
  - Accents: Soft gold/amber tones
  - Natural: Warm wood, plants, neutral tones
  - Avoid: Cold blues, sterile whites, aggressive reds

**Composition Guidelines:**
- Rule of thirds—person/main subject NOT centered but balanced
- Negative space for text overlay (title will go on this image)
- Visual hierarchy: Human → Technology → Environment
- Depth: Use shallow depth of field to keep focus on human element

**Mood & Emotion:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Empowered, not overwhelmed
- Professional, not cold
- Forward-looking, not sci-fi
- Grounded, not fantastical
- Warm, not sterile

---

## Post-Generation Adjustments

After generating the image, consider these edits:

1. **Add subtle overlay gradient** (darker at edges) to ensure text readability
2. **Adjust contrast** to make title text pop
3. **Color grade** to match brand colors (teal/blue family)
4. **Crop for title placement** - ensure key visual elements won't be covered by:
   - "Ai First: Your Operating System for Human-Ai Productivity"
   - Subtitle text

---

## Brand Alignment Checklist

Before finalizing, verify the image conveys:

✅ **Human-centered** - People are the focus, not technology
✅ **Empowerment** - Subject looks capable, confident, in control
✅ **Authentic** - Real work environment, not staged corporate stock
✅ **Accessible** - Technology feels helpful, not intimidating
✅ **Professional** - Enterprise-appropriate, sophisticated
✅ **Warm** - Inviting and human, not cold or sterile
✅ **Progressive** - Forward-looking without being sci-fi
✅ **Behavioral** - Shows action/work/doing, not just observation

❌ **Avoid These:**
- Robots or robot hands
- Generic business handshakes
- People pointing at screens
- Obvious stock photography poses
- Overwhelming technology
- Isolation/loneliness
- Cold, blue-only color schemes
- Futuristic settings disconnected from reality

## Recommended Approach

**Best Practice:** Generate 3-4 variations using different prompts above, then:

1. Test each with your title text overlaid
2. Get feedback from 2-3 team members
3. Ensure the winning image:
   - Doesn't compete with text for attention
   - Reinforces the "human-centered" message
   - Feels appropriate for C-suite audience
   - Works across different projection settings (light/dark rooms)

**Pro Tip:** The "Primary Image Prompt" (Option 1) balances professionalism with the Ai First brand message most effectively for enterprise sales contexts.
