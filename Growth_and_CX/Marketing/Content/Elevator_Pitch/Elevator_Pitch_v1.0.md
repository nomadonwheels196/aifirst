## How It Works: The Ai First Framework

A closed-loop system designed to solve the two biggest barriers to AI transformation: **adoption** and **competency**.

**1. Shadow** – Observe and map how people work across tools and contexts

**2. Assess** – Measure each person's current Ai adoption and competency; assess company strategy, challenges, approved tools, and their role

**3. Plan** – Build a tailored roadmap that maximizes productivity and business impact through smarter Ai utilization

**4. Demonstrate Value** – Earn trust first. Recommend targeted use cases aligned with their goals. Quantify time saved, quality improved, creativity unlocked—trigger the "lightbulb moments" that drive adoption

**5. Enable** – Deliver step-by-step, in-workflow coaching so users learn by doing, not by training

**6. Iterate** – Continuously learn and refine both individual plans and the broader organizational model

**This cycle transforms both people and performance** — raising Ai fluency, embedding adoption into daily work, and creating measurable, compounding ROI.
