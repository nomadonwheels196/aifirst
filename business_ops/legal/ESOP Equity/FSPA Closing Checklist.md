# Founder Stock Purchase — Closing Checklist

Document set
- Founder Stock Purchase Agreement (restricted common; reverse vesting)
- Board Consent approving founder issuances and EIP adoption
- 83(b) election packet(s) (one per founder or early exerciser)
- Securities legends and escrow instructions for unvested shares

Funding & records
- Wire founder purchase price to company account (par/nominal)
- Issue share certificates or electronic entries; update cap table (as‑converted 1:1)
- File 83(b) within 30 days; retain certified mail proof and Company copy

Compliance notes
- Reverse vesting: 4y/1y cliff; company repurchase at issue price
- QSBS: if pursuing Delaware parent path, founder equity at original issuance starts 5‑year clock
- 409A: grant options (if any) at FMV; refresh valuation at material events or 12 months
