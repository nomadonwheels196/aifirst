## Ai‑First Inc. (Delaware C‑Corp)

The undersigned, being all of the members of the Board of Directors (the "Board") of **Ai‑First Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), hereby adopt the following resolutions by unanimous written consent pursuant to Section 141(f) of the Delaware General Corporation Law ("DGCL") and the Company’s Bylaws.

### 1. Confirmation of Incorporation; Bylaws
RESOLVED, that the Board acknowledges the filing of the Company’s Certificate of Incorporation with the Delaware Secretary of State and hereby adopts the Bylaws presented to the Board, with such changes as any officer may approve.

### 2. Appointment of Officers
RESOLVED, that the following individuals are appointed to the offices set forth opposite their names, to serve at the pleasure of the Board and until their successors are duly elected and qualified or until their earlier resignation or removal:

| Name | Office |
| --- | --- |
| Travis Sheppard | Chief Executive Officer |
| [Name] | Chief Technology Officer |

### 3. Approval of Founder Stock Issuances; Consideration; Legends
RESOLVED, that the Company is authorized to issue to each founder named below the number of shares of the Company’s Common Stock set forth opposite such founder’s name, for the consideration set forth below, pursuant to a Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement in substantially the form presented to the Board, which includes reverse vesting and Company repurchase rights at original issue price for unvested shares:

| Founder | Shares | Price per Share | Total Consideration |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Travis Sheppard | [X] | $0.0001 | $[ ] |
| [Founder 2] | [Y] | $0.0001 | $[ ] |

RESOLVED FURTHER, that the certificates or book‑entry statements representing such shares shall bear appropriate legends noting restrictions on transfer, the Company’s repurchase rights, and applicable securities law legends.

### 4. Vesting; Repurchase Rights
RESOLVED, that the shares issued to the founders shall be subject to a four (4) year vesting schedule with a one (1) year cliff (25% at the first anniversary, remainder monthly over 36 months), and the Company shall have the irrevocable right to repurchase any unvested shares at the original issue price upon termination of service, as set forth in the Restricted Stock Purchase Agreements.

### 5. 83(b) Election Notices
RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are authorized and directed to provide each US taxpayer founder with information necessary to complete an election under Section 83(b) of the Internal Revenue Code and to request a copy of any such election for the Company’s records, with the understanding that the filing obligation rests with the founder and the statutory 30‑day deadline is not extendable.

### 6. Securities Law Compliance; Blue Sky
RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are authorized to take any and all actions, and to prepare, execute, and deliver any and all documents, necessary or advisable to comply with applicable federal and state securities laws in connection with the issuance of shares to the founders, including reliance on available exemptions from registration.

### 7. Valuation Policy; 409A Planning
RESOLVED, that for purposes of setting fair market value for equity grants to US taxpayers, the Company shall obtain and periodically update independent valuations intended to qualify for safe‑harbor treatment under IRC §409A; and the officers are authorized to engage a qualified valuation firm and to adopt a grant‑timing process consistent with such valuations.

### 8. General Authorization
RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are authorized and directed to do any and all things and to execute and deliver all such instruments and documents as they may deem necessary or advisable to carry out the purposes and intent of the foregoing resolutions, any such actions to be conclusive evidence of such authority.
