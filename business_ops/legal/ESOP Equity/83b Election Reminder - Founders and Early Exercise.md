# 83(b) Election Reminder — Founders / Early Exercise

- File within 30 days of the stock issuance/early exercise date (hard deadline; no extensions).
- Include: completed IRS 83(b) form, cover letter, and self‑addressed stamped envelope; send via certified mail.
- Value: use the actual price paid; if par/nominal, include total paid and total shares.
- Copies: keep proof of mailing; provide a copy to the Company and your tax advisor.
- State equivalents: check if your state requires a copy.
