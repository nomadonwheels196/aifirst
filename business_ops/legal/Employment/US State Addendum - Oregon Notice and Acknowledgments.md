# State-Specific Employment Addendum — Oregon

Created by: Travis Sheppard
Category: HR & Employment (US — OR)
Status: Template for Legal Review

This Oregon Addendum supplements the Employment Agreement for Employees whose principal place
of work is Oregon. If there is a conflict, this Addendum controls to the extent required by
Oregon law.

1) Oregon Sick Leave (ORS 653.601–653.661)
- Accrual at least 1 hour per 30 hours worked; up to 40 hours/year; carryover and usage per
  state and applicable city ordinances.

2) Pay Equity Act; Wage Range Transparency
- Compliance with Oregon Equal Pay Act; upon request, disclosure of pay range for the
  position.

3) Wage Statements and Pay Frequency
- Company will provide itemized wage statements and comply with pay frequency rules.

4) Non‑Compete (ORS 653.295)
- Non‑competes are enforceable only if statutory conditions (including income thresholds and
  notice) are met; otherwise void. Company primarily relies on confidentiality, trade
  secrets, and non‑solicitation.

5) Meal and Rest Breaks; Overtime
- Compliance with Oregon meal/rest rules and overtime requirements.

6) Venue; Governing Law
- Oregon employment protections apply; venue for employment disputes may be in OR courts where
  required by law.
