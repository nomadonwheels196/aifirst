# State-Specific Employment Addendum — Washington

Created by: Travis Sheppard
Category: HR & Employment (US — WA)
Status: Template for Legal Review

This Washington Addendum supplements the Employment Agreement for Employees whose principal
place of work is Washington State. If there is a conflict, this Addendum controls to the
extent required by Washington law.

1) Paid Sick Leave (RCW 49.46.210)
- Accrual at least 1 hour per 40 hours worked; carryover of at least 40 hours to the next
  year; usage and notice per statute.

2) Equal Pay and Opportunities Act (EPOA) — Pay Transparency (RCW 49.58.110)
- For job postings, the Company will disclose wage scale/salary range and a general
  description of benefits and other compensation.

3) Wage Statements and Pay Frequency
- Company will provide itemized wage statements and comply with pay frequency rules.

4) Non‑Compete (RCW 49.62)
- Non‑competes are restricted to workers above statutory earnings thresholds, require advance
  notice, and are generally limited to 18 months. Company primarily relies on
  confidentiality, trade secrets, and non‑solicitation.

5) Meal and Rest Breaks; Overtime
- Compliance with WA meal/rest rules and overtime under the Washington Minimum Wage Act.

6) Venue; Governing Law
- Washington employment protections apply; venue for employment disputes may be in WA courts
  where required by law.
