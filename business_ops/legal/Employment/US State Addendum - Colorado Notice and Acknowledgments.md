# State-Specific Employment Addendum — Colorado

Created by: Travis Sheppard
Category: HR & Employment (US — CO)
Status: Template for Legal Review

This Colorado Addendum supplements the Employment Agreement for Employees whose principal place
of work is Colorado. If there is a conflict, this Addendum controls to the extent required by
Colorado law.

1) Equal Pay for Equal Work Act (EPEWA)
- Company will include compensation range and general benefits in job postings and promotion
  notices, and will provide required notice of promotional opportunities to all eligible
  employees.

2) Healthy Families and Workplaces Act (HFWA)
- Paid sick leave accrues at least 1 hour per 30 hours worked, up to 48 hours/year minimum,
  with carryover as required. Public health emergency leave will be provided as applicable.

3) Wage Transparency and Wage Statements
- Company will comply with CO wage statement requirements and anti‑retaliation provisions for
  wage transparency.

4) Non‑Compete (C.R.S. §8‑2‑113)
- Non‑compete covenants are restricted to highly compensated workers with advance notice.
  Company primarily relies on confidentiality, trade secrets, and non‑solicitation.

5) Breaks and Overtime
- Rest and meal break compliance per Colorado Overtime and Minimum Pay Standards (COMPS) Order;
  overtime as required by COMPS.
