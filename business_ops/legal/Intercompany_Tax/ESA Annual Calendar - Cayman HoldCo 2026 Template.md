## Governance & CIGA Milestones
- Q1: Board Meeting in Cayman (IP strategy; approve IP assignments/licensing)
- Q2: CIGA evidencing (meeting minutes, IP roadmap approvals); office lease/serviced office review
- Q3: Board Meeting in Cayman (licensing approvals > USD $100k; risk review)
- Q4: ESA documentation compile; budget for Cayman expenditure (7–10% of royalty revenue target)

## Filings & Documentation
- ESA Return: File with Cayman Registrar by [deadline per Registrar]  
- Maintain: Board minutes with Cayman attendance; IP strategy docs; licensing negotiations and approvals; invoices for Cayman office and local providers; payroll/contract for Cayman‑based employee or service provider

## Roles & Responsibilities
- Group CFO: ESA owner; ensures resources and expenditure; signs ESA return  
- Cayman Registered Office Provider: Monitors deadlines; assists in filings; maintains statutory books  
- Board Chair/Director: Schedules and attends Cayman board meetings; ensures CIGA conducted in Cayman

## Review & Audit
- Annual ESA compliance review with Cayman counsel  
- Update Intercompany Agreement §2.5 if requirements change  
- Maintain evidence for at least 6 years
