# Annex A — EU Standard Contractual Clauses (Reference)

Created by: Travis Sheppard
Category: Privacy & Compliance — GDPR
Status: Reference for Intercompany Agreement §6

This Annex incorporates by reference the EU Commission Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
adopted by Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914, including:
- Module Two (Controller to Processor)
- Module Three (Processor to Processor)

Link: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dec_impl/2021/914/oj

The parties agree that, for each intercompany data flow from Ai‑First Spain, S.L. to a
non‑EEA entity, the appropriate Module will apply, and the SCC annexes will be completed with
processing details (categories of data, purposes, duration, technical and organizational
measures). Any updates to the SCCs are deemed incorporated upon mutual written acknowledgment.
