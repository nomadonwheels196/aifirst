# ESA Evidence Checklist — Ai‑First Holdings Ltd. (Cayman)

Use this checklist to maintain contemporaneous evidence of Economic Substance (ESA) for the
Cayman HoldCo. Store artifacts in the ESA file and reference meeting minutes where relevant.

## Board & CIGA
- [ ] Board agendas/minutes for Cayman meetings (≥2 per year) with attendance logs marking
      in‑person Cayman presence
- [ ] Decisions on IP strategy (roadmap approvals), licensing, risk — noted as CIGA items
- [ ] Approvals of material IP filings/renewals and intercompany licenses (> USD 100k)

## Facilities & Services
- [ ] Registered office agreement and meeting room bookings (Cayman)
- [ ] Corporate services provider engagement letter; scope of services; invoices
- [ ] Any local staffing agreements (if applicable)

## Financial & Operations
- [ ] Royalty/services invoices; WHT filings and certificates (US Forms 1042/1042‑S; Spain 216/296)
- [ ] TP benchmarking extracts; quarterly true‑up working papers
- [ ] ESA return copy and filing confirmation

## Security & Data Transfers
- [ ] GDPR SCC Annex I/II/III (executed); TIA completed and approved by DPO
- [ ] Security KPIs/metrics; incident response logs (if any)

Owner: Company Secretary / CFO  
Review cadence: Quarterly; pre‑ESA filing
