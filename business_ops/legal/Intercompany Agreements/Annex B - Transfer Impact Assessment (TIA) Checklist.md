# Annex B — Transfer Impact Assessment (TIA) Checklist (Template)

Created by: Travis Sheppard
Category: Privacy & Compliance — GDPR
Status: Template for Intercompany Agreement §6

For each EEA→non‑EEA transfer, complete the following items:

1. Data Mapping: Identify data categories, purposes, data subjects, frequency, recipients.  
2. Legal Assessment: Assess destination country laws on government access (e.g., US FISA 702,
   EO 12333; Cayman laws) and redress mechanisms.  
3. Risk Analysis: Evaluate likelihood of access; consider nature of data and sensitivity.  
4. Supplementary Measures: Technical (encryption/pseudonymization), organizational, contractual
   measures applied; key management location.  
5. Residual Risk: Document residual risk and rationale for proceeding or alternatives.  
6. Approvals: DPO/legal review sign‑off; business owner approval; review date.  
7. Review Cycle: Reassess at least annually or upon legal/operational changes.
