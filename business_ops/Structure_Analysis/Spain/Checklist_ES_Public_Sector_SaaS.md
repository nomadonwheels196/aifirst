# Checklist — Spain Public Sector SaaS Requirements (Baseline)

Company/Registration
- Legal entity eligible to contract (ES/EU/foreign) with representation in Spain as needed.
- ROLECE registration (recommended) or equivalent documentation for solvency/eligibility.
- Digital certificate(s) for legal representative to sign submissions on PLACSP.

Evidence templates
- `company/rolece/ROLECE_evidence.md` (screenshot/certificate, registry data)
- `company/powers/Power_of_Attorney_ES_en.md` (bilingual template)
- `company/certificates/Tax_Social_Security_Compliance.md` (how to request from AEAT/TGSS)

Security (ENS)
- Determine ENS level applicable to data/services (Básico/Medio/Alto).
- Evidence: ENS certificate (ENAC-accredited) or declaration if accepted by pliego; scope includes SaaS/service.
- Mappings: ISO 27001/27017/27018, SOC 2 (if present) mapped to ENS controls.

Evidence templates
- `security/ens/ENS_Statement_of_Applicability.md`
- `security/ens/ENS_Implementation_Plan.md`
- `security/iso/ISO27001_Certificate.pdf` (placeholder)

Interoperability (ENI)
- Use accepted formats/signatures; comply with electronic records and API/data exchange rules when applicable.
- Evidence: technical statement, conformance mapping, sample artifacts.

Evidence templates
- `interoperability/ENI_Conformance_Statement.md`
- `interoperability/Signature_Examples_PAdES_XAdES.md`

Accessibility (RD 1112/2018, EN 301 549)
- Conform to WCAG 2.1 AA via EN 301 549.
- Evidence: Accessibility Conformance Report (ACR), testing procedures, remediation plan.

Evidence templates
- `accessibility/ACR_EN301549_WCAG21AA.md`
- `accessibility/Accessibility_Test_Plan.md`

Data Protection (GDPR/LOPDGDD)
- DPA (encargo de tratamiento) aligned with pliego terms; subprocessor list; SCCs if international transfers.
- Evidence: template DPA, TOMs summary, records of processing, DPIA (if needed).

Evidence templates
- `privacy/DPA_Template_ES_en.md`
- `privacy/TOMs_Summary.md`
- `privacy/Subprocessors_List.md`
- `privacy/DPIA_Template.md`

Procurement Operations
- PLACSP account and process readiness (submission, questions, clarifications).
- Bid bonds/guarantees if requested; bank instrument readiness.
- Language: Spanish deliverables; certified translations if required.

Evidence templates
- `procurement/PLACSP_Submission_Checklist.md`
- `procurement/QandA_Log_Template.md`
- `procurement/Guarantee_Bank_Instrument_Spec.md`

Invoicing/Tax
- Facturae e-invoicing capability via FACe.
- Evidence: sample Facturae XML and FACe onboarding proof.

Evidence templates
- `invoicing/Facturae_Sample.xml`
- `invoicing/FACe_Onboarding_Notes.md`

Service/Contractual
- SLAs and support hours aligned with public sector expectations; penalties handling.
- Reversibility/exit clauses, data location/sovereignty statements.

Evidence templates
- `service/SLA_Template_ES_en.md`
- `service/Reversibility_and_Data_Location_Statement.md`

Notes
- Always defer to specific pliegos: they override the baseline.
