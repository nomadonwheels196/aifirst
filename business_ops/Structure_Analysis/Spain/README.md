# Spain Public Sector Procurement — Starter Pack

This folder centralizes Spain-specific research for selling SaaS to Spanish public administrations. It explains Spanish concepts in plain English, mapped to their Spanish names, and tracks sources and decisions.

Contents
- `Glossary_ES_Public_Procurement.md` — English definitions mapped to Spanish terms.
- `Research_Plan.md` — Methodology, scope, and deliverables.
- `Research_Log.md` — Sources, notes, and findings with citations.
- `Checklist_ES_Public_Sector_SaaS.md` — Baseline requirements and evidence list.

Scope
- Baseline: central government (AGE) requirements and platforms.
- Extensible: autonomous communities and local entities when relevant.

Status
- Initial scaffolding complete. Live research in progress.
