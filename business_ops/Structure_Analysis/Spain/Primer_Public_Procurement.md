# Primer — How Spanish Public Procurement Works (English with Spanish terms)

1) Discover opportunities (PLACSP)
- Where: Plataforma de Contratación del Sector Público (PLACSP).
- What you see: tender notices (anuncios) and the RFP package (pliegos).

2) Read the pliegos (binding docs)
- Administrative Terms (PCAP): rules, solvency requirements, award criteria, guarantees, contract terms.
- Technical Specs (PPT/PPTP): scope of work, technical requirements, service levels, certifications.

3) Check eligibility and solvency
- Economic solvency (solvencia económica y financiera): e.g., turnover, insurance.
- Technical solvency (solvencia técnica o profesional): references, staff CVs, certifications (e.g., ENS/ISO).
- Classification (clasificación): only required for certain works/services; often not needed for SaaS.

4) Register and prepare to submit
- ROLECE (Registro de Licitadores): optional but helpful registration proving solvency/eligibility.
- Digital certificates (certificados cualificados): for the legal representative to sign and submit via PLACSP.

5) Submit questions and the bid
- Questions/clarifications (consultas/clarificaciones) window opens before the submission deadline.
- Submit documentation (sobre/s): may be split (administrative, technical, economic) depending on the procedure.

6) Evaluation and award
- Evaluation committee (mesa de contratación) reviews solvency and technical proposal.
- Award criteria (criterios de adjudicación): price and quality factors (MEAT).
- Award (adjudicación) and formalization (formalización) of the contract.

7) Execute and invoice
- Contract execution (ejecución del contrato) per PCAP/PPT terms.
- E-invoicing (Facturae) through FACe; maintain compliance with ENS/ENI/accessibility/data protection obligations.

Key frameworks likely to appear
- ENS (Esquema Nacional de Seguridad): information security; level Básico/Medio/Alto.
- ENI (Esquema Nacional de Interoperabilidad): formats, signatures, records.
- Accessibility (RD 1112/2018; EN 301 549): WCAG 2.1 AA alignment.
- GDPR/LOPDGDD: data processing agreements and TOMs.

Tip
- The pliegos rule. Always align your evidence and commitments to what the PCAP and PPT require.
