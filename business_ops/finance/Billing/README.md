# Billing

Pricing, plan catalogs, invoicing flows, dunning strategy, and integrations (e.g., Stripe). Store diagrams, playbooks, and configuration docs (no secrets).

Subfolders
- Pricing — price books and change logs
- Plans — plan definitions and packaging
- Invoicing — invoice templates and numbering policy
- Dunning — retries, comms, and churn playbooks
- Integrations/Stripe — integration guides and field mappings
