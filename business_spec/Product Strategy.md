# Product Strategy

**Version:** v1.0

**Owner:** Travis Sheppard

---

## Product Thesis

**Ai First is the operating system for human-AI productivity.**

We turn every employee into a problem solver, every workflow into a learning system, and every company into a living example of how Ai and people can grow together.

Where most solutions focus on the *technology*, Ai First focuses on the *human behaviors* that unlock it. The platform is built to **observe how people work, coach them in real time, and measure the impact** — creating a self-improving loop of productivity, innovation, and empowerment.

---

## **Strategic Vision**

Ai First aims to make **Ai capability as universal as computer literacy** — not through training courses, but through experience.

We do this by embedding a **proactive Ai coach** across every level of the organization — from the individual contributor optimizing their day-to-day tasks to executives seeking measurable ROI on Ai investments.

The result: a new layer of organizational intelligence that sits between people, processes, and technology — connecting them through live, contextual feedback.

---

## **Core Engine: The Ai First Framework**

A closed-loop system that mirrors how people actually grow and learn at work — designed to solve the two biggest barriers to AI transformation: **adoption** and **competency**.

1. **Shadow** – Observe and map how people work across tools and contexts.
2. **Assess** – Measure each individual’s current level of AI adoption and competency within their existing workflows. We also assess company strategy, challenges, AI initiatives, approved tools, and the individual’s role — all as inputs to their personalized plan.
3. **Plan** – Build a tailored roadmap for each person that maximizes their productivity and business impact through smarter AI utilization.
4. **Demonstrate Value** – To increase adoption, we earn trust first. Based on each person’s motivations, we recommend targeted use cases, workflow improvements, and automations aligned with their goals and responsibilities. By quantifying time saved, quality improved, and creativity unlocked, we trigger the “lightbulb moments” that drive lasting adoption.
5. **Enable** – Deliver step-by-step, in-workflow coaching and support so users can learn by doing, not by training.
6. **Iterate** – Continuously learn from user data and refine both individual plans and the broader organizational model over time.

This cycle transforms both people and performance — **raising AI fluency, embedding adoption into daily work, and creating measurable, compounding ROI**.

---

## **Product Pillars**

### **1. Behavioral Coaching at Scale**

Ai First functions as a **personal AI coach** embedded directly into existing tools and workflows. It:

- Observes user patterns and identifies friction points.
- Suggests Ai interventions contextually (e.g., prompts, automations, workflow redesigns).
- Provides guided execution, not just recommendations.
- Tracks progress and celebrates wins to reinforce behavior change.

### **2. Human-Led Automation**

Rather than engineers building every workflow, Ai First empowers employees to **redesign workflows and own their own automations**.

- Low-code/No-code tools and natural-language interfaces make Ai accessible to non-technical users.
- Each automation can be shared, remixed, and improved by peers.
- Over time, organizations accumulate a living “Ai capability library.”

### **3. Contextual Intelligence Layer**

The platform learns how different teams, roles, and individuals work.

- Personalization ensures coaching feels relevant.
- Data flows safely within enterprise boundaries (privacy by design).
- Insights feed dashboards for leaders: adoption rates, time savings, innovation hotspots, and star players.

### **4. Continuous ROI Feedback**

Transformation only sticks when it’s measurable.

Ai First quantifies ROI at every level — individual, team, and enterprise — through metrics such as:

- Productivity lift per workflow
- Ai utilization rate
- Task automation percentage
- Time reallocation to high-value work

This builds a **shared language of progress** across leadership and frontline teams.

**Platform Architecture (Simplified)**

| **Layer** | **Purpose** |
| --- | --- |
| **Client Layer** | Cross-platform Ai coach accessible via desktop app, browser plugin, and integrations (Slack, Notion, M365, etc.) |
| **Behavior Engine** | Observes workflows, identifies friction, triggers contextual interventions |
| **Coaching Layer** | Conversational and visual guidance delivered via real-time overlay |
| **Knowledge Graph** | Maps tasks, skills, and tools to outcomes for adaptive learning |
| **Analytics & ROI Engine** | Captures usage data and quantifies individual + organizational impact |
| **Governance Layer** | Ensures privacy, compliance, and safe use of Ai within company policies |
