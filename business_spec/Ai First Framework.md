## Core Engine: Ai First Framework

A closed feedback loop: users grow → enterprise gets measurable ROI → platform learns and improves.

1. **Shadow** – Observe how people work

2. **Assess** – Identify where Ai can help

3. **Plan** – Recommend improvements of Ai usage and tools

4. **Demonstrate Value** – Show the individual/company value of leveraging Ai in the right ways

5. **Enable** – Coach user step-by-step

6. **Iterate** – Continuously refine
