# Recruiting

## Purpose
Source, assess, and hire candidates aligned to role requirements and values.

## Process
- Intake: role, level, competencies, JD
- Sourcing: channels and outreach
- Interview: stages, scorecards, debriefs
- Offer: approval path and comp bands

## Artifacts
- `Job_Descriptions/` — templates and role-specific JDs

## Security & Privacy
- Candidate PII belongs in ATS; link to records, don’t copy.
